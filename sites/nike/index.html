Our Story

Born
to
Run

In 1964, Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman had an idea: make the world's best running shoes. What started in a garage in Oregon became a global force that redefined sport, culture, and style.

The Swoosh became more than a logo. It became a symbol of ambition, of breaking through limits, of every runner, athlete, dreamer, and creator who dared to push further. From the courts to the streets, from the track to the runway.

Today, Nike Streetwear carries that same DNA. Engineered for movement. Designed for the city. Built for those who refuse to stay still.
