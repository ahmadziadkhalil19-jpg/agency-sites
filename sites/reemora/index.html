Our Story

Born from a
Passion for Light

In a small studio overlooking the Mediterranean, two designers became obsessed with capturing that fleeting moment when the sun dips below the horizon and the world turns gold.

They spent three years perfecting the optics, testing over 200 color combinations, and refining the industrial design until every angle felt effortless.

REEMORA was born not as a lamp, but as an experience — a daily reminder that beauty exists in the simplest of things.

Elena Vasquez

Co-Founder & Creative Director
