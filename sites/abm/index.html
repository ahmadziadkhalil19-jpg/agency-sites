Our Philosophy

Born from a belief in
intentional living

In 2019, our founder stood before an overflowing dresser and realized something profound: the chaos of unfolded clothes was a mirror for the chaos of modern life. ABM was born from a simple, radical idea — that the act of folding, done with intention, could transform not just your wardrobe, but your state of mind.

Every ABM product is the result of obsessive research and a refusal to compromise. We source aerospace-grade materials, engineer to sub-millimeter tolerances, and obsess over the sensory experience of the fold — the snap, the alignment, the quiet satisfaction of order restored.
